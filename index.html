About

What is J Fire?

J Fire is a lightweight, browser-based budgeting tool designed for clinical research coordinators and finance teams. It runs entirely in the browser — no server, no database, no subscription.

Data entered is stored only in your browser's localStorage and is never transmitted anywhere. Sign up or sign in above to access the full budgeting features.
